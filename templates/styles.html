CSS

Attribute values
color the colour of the text. This can be hexadecimal (#000000 for black, #ffffff for white, #00ffff for yellow etc) or one of the preset colours(black, red, ...)
background-color The colour of the background.Same colours as color
margin How much gap there is around the border to the other elementsUsually expressed in pixels. You can specify top, right, bottom and left in the one expression
border The size, colour and type of border around the elementeg 2px solid #ffffff
padding the amount of space between the border and the elementUsually expressed in pixels (eg 3px)
